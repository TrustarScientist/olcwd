Lesson 5: Lists in HTML

📘 Topic Overview

Lists help us to show items that go together. For example, a list of your favorite snacks 🍫 or your school subjects 📚! There are two main types of lists in HTML:

  • Unordered Lists: These use bullet points.
  • Ordered Lists: These use numbers or letters.

There’s also a description list that shows items with explanations.
